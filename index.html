The company

Who we are.

Tasting & Toasting is being built to be a wine experience company , entering the European market through Spain.

We position ourselves as multi-functional. Wine education, curated kits, hosted tastings, event organization, sommelier engagement , these are the directions. Today, we’re in pre-launch and pre-licensing. No commercial wine activity yet.

The prototypes you see here are the design and product work we’ve done while the legal and operational foundation is being prepared. They’re a window into where we’re headed, not a live storefront.

If you’d like to be in touch , about wine, design, partnership, regulation, or just curiosity , write to us.
