Cookies are small text files that websites place on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit them. They allow the website to recognize your device on subsequent visits and to remember information about you, such as your preferences or login state.

"Cookies" in this Policy includes similar tracking technologies (web beacons, pixels, local storage, fingerprinting techniques) that may be used for the same purposes as cookies.

2. Legal Framework

Cookies on this website are governed by:

  • Article 22.2 of Spanish Law 34/2002 (LSSI-CE)

  • Articles 6 and 7 of GDPR (consent requirements)

  • AEPD Cookie Guidelines (most recent version)

  • European Data Protection Board Guidelines 5/2020 on consent

Non-essential cookies are activated only with your prior, informed, specific, and unambiguous consent.

Most browsers allow you to manage cookies through their settings. Instructions for major browsers:
