Carlos Mendoza

14 years across Rioja and Ribera del Duero. Fluent in Spanish, English, Italian.

Carlos started in his uncle's bodega in San Vicente de la Sonsierra at sixteen, washing barrels. Twenty-two years later he writes the wine list at La Buena Mesa Madrid, teaches at the Spanish Sommelier Academy, and runs vertical comparisons for our Salamanca center. Specializes in the difficult question , why this terroir, this year, this exact bottle.
